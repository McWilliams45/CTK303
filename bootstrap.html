Call of Duty: Black Ops 6

Developed by Treyarch and Raven, Black Ops 6 is a spy action thriller set in the early 90s, a period of transition and upheaval in global politics, characterized by the end of the Cold War and the rise of the United States as a single superpower. It features a Campaign, Multiplayer, and Zombies.

Marvel Rivals

Marvel Rivals is a Super Hero team-based PVP shooter! Assemble an all-star Marvel squad, devise countless strategies by combining powers to form unique Team-Up skills and fight in destructible, ever-changing battlefields across the continually evolving Marvel Universe!

Fortnite

Fortnite is a third person shooter survival video game where the player has to survive against up to 100 other players unless you are looking to play the mode Reload which has 40 players.
